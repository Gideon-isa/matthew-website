Popular Bible Quotes




John 3:16
For God so loved the world, that he gave his only begotten Son the whosoever belives in him should not perish, but have evalasting life.

God loves us equally as his own beloved son.



John 14:12
Verily verily i say unto you he that belives on me, the works that I do shall he do also; and greater works than these shall he do; because I go unto the Father.



Beliving on Jesus makes you able to do the type of works he did here on earth and even more.



Hebrews 11:1
Now faith is the substance of things hoped for the evidence of things not seen.



Faith is based on things not seen



Hebrews 4:12
For the Word is quick, and powerful, and sharper than any two-edged sword, piercing even to the diving asunder of soul and spirit, and of the joints and marrow, and is a discerner of the thouhts and intents of the heart.



Only the word of God is able to seperate an inserperable things such as our spirit from our soul and also knows the hidden things such as our thought. Meaning the word can do the impossible things.



Acts 1:8
But you shall receive power, after that the Holy Ghost is come upon you: and you shall be witness unto me both in Jerusalem, and in all Judaea, and in Samaria, and unto the uttermost part of the earth.



The power or ability to witness about God comes to us when the Holy Ghost has come into our lives. The Holy Ghost gives the power to be able to behave and do things God can only do.
